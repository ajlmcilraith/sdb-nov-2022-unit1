Cats: Aliens?

Aliens are all around us. They take many forms. They have unknown agendas. They sometimes take the forms of cats. We do not know why. It us possible that aliens enjoy cat food and skritches. This is only conjecture, however, as the percentage of cats which are in fact aliens is unknown, so the behavior of one cannot be said to be defined in reference to the other.

Aliens are known to share a hatred of velociraptors.
