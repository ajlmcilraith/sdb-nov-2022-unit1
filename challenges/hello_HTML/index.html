In July of 2001, one human being realized a profound truth: cats, dinosaurs, and aliens, are inextricably linked. This is their sordid tale.

it begins with a cat. a seemingly ordinary animal, yet one which has a strange fondness for certain activities that some might call suspicious.

  1. They like to knock things off of raised horizontal surfaces, despite the fact that this is only minorly amusing
  2. They scream to be let outside, yet refuse to move
  3. They are inconcievably adorable

all of this evidence points to one conclusion: cats are not what they appear. Many could in fact be extra-terrestrials.
